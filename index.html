Ambisonics domain Singing Voice Separation combining Deep Neural Network and Direction Aware Multichannel NMF


This page contains separation results for article "Ambisonics domain Singing Voice Separation combining Deep Neural Network and Direction Aware Multichannel NMF" submitted to the IEEE 23rd Workshop on Multimedia Signal Processing.

Authors: Antonio J. Munoz-Montoro, Julio J. Carabias-Orti and Pedro Vera-Candeas

Datasets

The experimental evaluation was carried out on the Demixing Secret Dataset (DSD100). A subset of 50 mixtures has been used for the training process together with their corresponding individual sources, and another 50 mixtures for the evaluation.
